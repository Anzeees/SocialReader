Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

1. Who we are

SocialReader is a personal, non-commercial educational project that helps users keep track of their readings and share reviews within a private environment.

2. What data we collect

  • Email address and password (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Display name or username.
  • Optional data such as reading lists, reviews, ratings, and favorites.

3. How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your access to the application.
  • Store your progress, lists, and reviews.
  • We do not share your data with third parties or use it for commercial purposes.

4. Where your data is stored

All data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud), under Google’s privacy and security policies.

5. How we protect your information

We use HTTPS and Firebase’s secure services to protect your information. Only the authenticated user has access to their own data.

6. User rights

You may request account deletion and data removal at any time by emailing: support@socialreader.com (simulated contact).

7. Contact

For any questions, feel free to reach out to our support email: support@socialreader.com.
